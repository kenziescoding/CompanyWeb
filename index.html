Basics

Greetings & People

Bonjour! or Salut! Means Hello. Comment vas-tu? Means, How are you? Coucou! Means, Hey there! Bon matin & Bonne nuit. Means, Good Morning & Good night. Comment t'appelles is Whats your name? When you are learning french, try to recognize or get used to what each word means. Great Job! You just got done with lesson one. Reread this one more time and come back soon to study more! This lesson was to learn the basic greetings.
Next is pronouns:
Nous! Means We and Us. Elles and Leur! Means they and them. Elle and Son! Means She and Her. il and lui, Means He and Him. Les Lerurs! Means Theirs. Congrats! You've gotten so far! Repeat the same steps in Lesson one for all the following lessons. Now that we are done with pronouns,
Next is people:
Maman, means Mom. Père! Means Dad, Father, & Papa. Frère Means, Brother, Bro, & sibling. Soeur! Means Sister, sis, & sibling. Grand-mère, is Grandma, Granny, Nana, Gran, Grammy, & Grandmother. Grand-père is Grandfather, Grampa, Grandad, & Gramp. Ami, means Friend, Dear, Buddy, Pal, & Mate. Meilleur ami is Best friend, Bestie, & Best. Petit ami is Boyfriend, Lover, & Mate. Copine means Girlfriend, & Lover.
